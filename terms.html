Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This document outlines how we handle your data, images, and personal information to provide you with the best AI-powered fashion experience.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By downloading, accessing, or using the SpotStyle mobile application ("Service"), you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of these terms, please do not use the Service.

2. User Data, Image Storage & Biometric Consent

⚠️ Explicit Consent for Image & Body Data Storage: By uploading photos to SpotStyle, you explicitly grant us permission to store and process images containing faces, body types, and clothing information on our secure cloud servers.

To provide our AI analysis and outfit identification features, we collect and store the following data:

  • Uploaded Images: Photographs or screenshots containing outfits, which may include your face, body type, and physical features.
  • AI-Extracted Data: Bounding boxes, item tags (e.g., "jacket", "shoes"), and style ratings generated by our AI.
  • Account Info: Username, email, and style preferences.

How We Use This Data

Your images and body type data are used exclusively to:

  • Identify clothing items using Google Cloud Vision API.
  • Provide personalized outfit recommendations and style ratings.
  • Match your body type to clothing for better fit recommendations.
  • Display outfits in your personal "Spotted Feed" mood board.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored in our Supabase backend database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and images from unauthorized access. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support.

3. AI Processing & Third-Party APIs

SpotStyle relies on third-party services to process images and fetch product data. By using our app, you acknowledge that your uploaded images and extracted data are processed by the following services:

  • Google Cloud Vision API: Used to scan uploaded images, draw bounding boxes around clothing items, and tag pieces. (Free tier: 1,000 image scans/month).
  • Affiliate APIs (Amazon Associates, AWIN, ShareASale): Used to fetch real product data, prices, and affiliate links based on the AI tags extracted from your images.

We do not sell your image data or body type information to third parties. Data shared with APIs is strictly for the purpose of generating your fashion results.

4. Subscriptions & Billing (Pro Tier)

SpotStyle offers a "Full Companion Pro" subscription for $4.99/month, processed securely via Stripe. By subscribing, you agree to the following:

  • Billing occurs monthly until cancellation.
  • A 14-day free trial may be offered; standard billing begins after the trial ends.
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time directly in your app store settings.
  • Pro features (Style Rating, unlimited saves, Share a Spot) will be locked upon cancellation.

5. Affiliate Disclosure

SpotStyle participates in affiliate programs with Amazon Associates, AWIN, and ShareASale. When you click an affiliate link in our app and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence which products we display, as our AI matches are based purely on visual similarity and your budget preferences.

6. Limitation of Liability

SpotStyle provides fashion identification and shopping suggestions for entertainment and convenience purposes only. We do not guarantee the accuracy of AI identifications, the availability of products, or the sizing/fit of recommended items. We are not liable for any purchases made through third-party retailers.

Our Roadmap

The SpotStyle Build Plan

Here is exactly how we are bringing this to life step-by-step to ensure a seamless AI-powered fashion experience.

01

The Brain (Supabase Backend)

Where we are today.

We set up a free database to store users, uploaded outfits, AI tags, and shopping links. This builds the "memory" of SpotStyle—a secure place in the cloud to catch photos, remember who uploaded them, and store the shopping links attached to them.

02

The Body (React Native & Expo Frontend)

The mobile app shell.

We build the mobile app shell implementing the dark luxury theme (near-black background, blush pink accents, Cormorant Garamond fonts) and build the camera/photo upload screen so users can start snapping their outfits.

03

The Eyes (Google Cloud Vision API)

Connecting to Google’s AI.

When a user uploads a photo, the AI will scan it, draw bounding boxes around the jacket, shoes, and bag, and send those tags back to your database.

Free: 1,000 scans/mo
Scalable: ~$1.50 per 2,000 scans
04

The Wallet (Affiliate APIs)

Monetization & matching.

We take those AI tags and ping Amazon Associates and AWIN to fetch real products, prices, and your affiliate links to display in the app. It is completely free to sign up as a "publisher." They pay you a commission when users buy.
