What It Does

Core Features

📸
Feature 01
Snap & Identify

Point your camera at anyone wearing anything. The AI breaks the outfit into individual pieces — jacket, shoes, bag, jeans, accessories — and identifies each one separately. Not just "similar vibes" — actual product matches.

🛒
Feature 02
One-Tap Shop

Every identified item links directly to where you can buy it. Multiple retailers shown side by side so you can pick the best price. Cashback and discount codes surfaced automatically.

💸
Feature 03
Budget Alternatives

Can't afford the exact item? SpotStyle automatically finds lookalikes at 3 different price points — luxury, mid-range, and budget — so no one is priced out.

👤
Feature 04
Your Style Profile

One-time setup: body type, preferred style, budget range. Every result is then filtered through your lens — not just "here's the item" but "here's how it'd look on someone built like you."

💾
Feature 05
Spotted Feed

Save every outfit you spot into a personal mood board. Organise by occasion, season, or vibe. Think Pinterest — but built around real-world things you actually saw and want.

🔗
Feature 06
Share a Spot

Found an incredible outfit? Share the full breakdown — every piece, every price — as one link. Friends can shop the exact same look directly from your share.

📱
Feature 07
Import from Anywhere

Screenshot from TikTok, Instagram, Netflix, or anywhere else — drag it into SpotStyle and it works the same as a live photo. This kills Screenshop's workflow.

⭐
Feature 08
Style Rating

After identifying an outfit, AI tells you why it works — the colour theory, the silhouette logic, the occasion fit — so you actually learn style, not just copy it.

The Plan

Phase-by-Phase Roadmap

P1
Validate Weeks 1–4 $0
  • Download Screenshop and Shop the Look — use them every single day for a week
  • Keep a doc of every frustration, every failed search, every clunky moment
  • Post in r/femalefashionadvice, r/streetwear, r/malefashionadvice — ask what frustrates people about finding outfits IRL
  • Let real users write your product spec for free
P2
Build MVP Weeks 5–16 $18K–$30K or DIY
  • Core snap-and-identify feature only — nothing else
  • Connect to 3 affiliate retailers (Amazon, ASOS, Zara)
  • Basic saved feed so users can bookmark looks
  • iOS first — Android after you have traction
  • No style profile yet — keep the scope ruthlessly small
P3
Launch & Test Week 17 ~$500
  • Post on TikTok: "I saw this outfit on the street → found every piece in 10 seconds" — this format is made for virality
  • Submit to Product Hunt on a Tuesday
  • DM 20 fashion micro-influencers (10k–100k followers) — offer free Pro access for an honest review
  • Post the comparison table (SpotStyle vs competitors) in fashion subreddits
P4
Monetise Month 4+ Revenue starts
  • Affiliate commissions active from day one — 5–15% of every purchase made through your links
  • Add $4.99/month Pro tier: unlocks style profile, shareable look links, and unlimited spotted feed
  • 1,000 users × 1 purchase/month × $60 avg × 8% commission = $4,800/month passive
P5
Full Build Month 6+ Funded by revenue
  • Use affiliate income to fund the budget alternatives engine
  • Build the style profile and body-type filtering system
  • Launch the Share a Spot social layer
  • By this point you have real user data telling you exactly what to build next — no guessing
